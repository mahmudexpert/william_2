Fixed-Price Model (FP)

This model is based on a pre-determined estimation of hours and a clearly defined scope of work. We calculate a comprehensive price from this estimation. Once the contract is agreed upon, the price remains fixed, ensuring no additional billing for extra work, provided there are no changes to the agreed technical specifications.

Timing & Meterial Pricing Model (T&M)

Under the T&M model, clients are billed for the actual time spent by the contractor on the project, along with any material costs incurred. This model focuses on payment for the process rather than the final product, offering unparalleled flexibility. Work is executed in iterative stages, allowing for ongoing adjustments and refinements to align with evolving project needs.

Mixed Pricing Model

Hybrid Pricing Model Combining the best of both worlds, the Hybrid Model merges the structure of Fixed-Price with the flexibility of Time and Material (T&M). This approach is ideal when you're working within a specific timeline yet dealing with evolving or uncertain requirements. It offers the ability to adjust the project scope dynamically, manage additional costs effectively, and facilitate regular project updates and revisions.
